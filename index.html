HOI AN: Craft Village Tour & Lantern Making Class

Explore nearby craft villages by waterway by boat.
Learn the traditional art of making lanterns.

On this tour, you can visit a traditional craft village by boat on the Thu Bồn River.

After pickup from Hội An, drop off at the port and
board the boat to Thanh Ha Pottery Village.

You can also visit Kim Bong Carpentry Village, famous for its traditional
woodworking products, and tour the woodworking workshops.

Afterwards, take a boat ride back to Thanh Ha Pottery Village, one of the oldest pottery
villages in Vietnam, where you can see how the villagers make pottery products.

Next, visit the Thanh Hà Terracotta Park Museum to see masterpieces made from clay,
such as the Leaning Tower of Pisa (Italy) and the Taj Mahal (India).

Finally, the tour concludes with a visit to a lantern craftsman to learn
the traditional craft of lantern making.

  • 5 NIGHTS, 5 - STAR
  • HOTEL, ALL RESEVED
  • $ 900
